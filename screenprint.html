Screenprinting Project: God is Great

The screen printing project was actually pretty fun. I learned how to saw wood the safe way and even had to do some sewing, which isn't really my thing. For the shared canvas, I wanted to make it about what helps me the most in life, so I made the project about God. There were a lot of steps to it, like figuring out our ideas, measuring wood, and stretching it out to fit the frame. Doing the vinyl cuts was super satisfying. Paint got everywhere which made it a mess, but that made it way more fun. Putting the woodwork vinyl and sewing all together made it a nice good and exciting project.
