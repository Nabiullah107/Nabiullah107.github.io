My Black Box

My project had a funnel connected by a tube to a bucket which has a hole. Another tube goes into another bucket with another tube going outside and can take all the water outside.

How its Made

  • tubes
  • glue guns
  • cardboard
  • buckets

Explanation

We built our box by using tube buckets, glue guns and cardboard boxes First we made holes in the buckets then we made the tubes to the right size then we connected the tubes to the buckets and we glued them together.
